In my freshman year, I collaborated with three peers in the project focused on the **conductivity of graphite and its potential application in seismic wave detection**. We earned Second Prize (Top 15%) in the National College Student Physics Innovation Experiment Competition.

Project: Seismic Wave Detection Experiment Based on Graphite Conductivity
======

We investigated the impact of **temperature, magnetic field, bending stress** on graphite conductivity, and the principles of various seismic wave detectors. Here is one image shows how we used four-probe method to measure the influence of temperature to graphite conductivity.
